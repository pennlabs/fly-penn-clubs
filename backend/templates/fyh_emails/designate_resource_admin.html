Hi Campus Partners,

We hope this email finds you well and that your semester is off to great start! As we experience increased traffic on Hub@Penn platform with the new semester, we are reaching out to all resources to ensure there is an active individual/team in charge of updating all information on your page. If someone on your team needs to be granted access to manage your page, we wanted to be sure you know exactly how to do that!

Granting access to edit your resource information is easy! Simply navigate to your resource on Hub@Penn and click the green "Manage Resource" button.

Then select the "Admin" tab.

In this tab you can send new email invites for access to the resource or update titles and permissions of current resource admins.

What is Hub@Penn?
Hub@Penn is a platform to help Penn students find and connect with university support resources. Whether you need help with financial wellness, academic support, healthcare, career and fellowship applications, and more, you can use Hub@Penn to find the resource that meets your needs.
