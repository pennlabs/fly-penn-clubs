[Hub@Penn] Update Your Penn Resource

Hi Campus Partners,

We hope this email finds you well!

As the new semester is upon us, we are writing with a reminder to update your resource listing on the Hub@Penn platform. Outdated information is a common complaint we hear from students! We want to ensure that Penn students have the most up-to-date information from all resources on the platform so that they can access relevant program events, mailing lists, FAQs, and contact information from your office.

Please review your current resource offering on the platform and make sure that all the information is correct.

If you need to designate a different member of your team to update your Hub@Penn listing, you can invite them as editors in the "Admin" tab.

We are excited to partner with you on the Hub@Penn initiative and create a centralized one-stop-shop for Penn student questions and concerns.

Please let us know if you have any questions.

Thank you!

Best,
Hub@Penn Admin

What is Hub@Penn?
Hub@Penn is a platform to help Penn students find and connect with university support resources. Whether you need help with financial wellness, academic support, healthcare, career and fellowship applications, and more, you can use Hub@Penn to find the resource that meets your needs.
