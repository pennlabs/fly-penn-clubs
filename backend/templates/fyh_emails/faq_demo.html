Better Utilize Your Hub@Penn Resource Page with FAQs

There is a lot of useful information on your resource page on Hub@Penn! One of the best ways to relay that information to the Penn Community is the Questions section. As users are exploring Hub@Penn, they are able to ask questions directly to your resource page which you can subsequently answer either privately to that user or publicly so others can see your response.

A great way to utilize this function is to prepopulate your Questions section with the resources’ most frequently asked questions. You are able to pose and answer questions on your own page to provide valuable information that users may ask about your resource.

We’ve prepared a short tutorial video which illustrates how to best utilize the Question section of your Hub@Penn resource page.
