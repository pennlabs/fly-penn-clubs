Thank you to everyone who has already completed your Hub@Penn resource page!

This platform comes at the request of Penn's senior administration and after close collaboration with NSOAI, OLI, Penn Labs, and VPUL. We look forward to offering Hub@Penn as a resource for Penn students starting in advance of the Spring 2021 semester.
