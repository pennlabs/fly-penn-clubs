Help Us Share a New Resource for Finding Penn Resources!

Hello!

Penn’s newest online spot for resources launched earlier this semester. Hub@Penn is a one-stop shop for all resources within the Penn community. Born out of a vision of easing access to Penn resources for all of Penn’s community, and made by Penn students, Hub@Penn is a great resource for all students. As we begin to anticipate the coming semester, we need your help!

As you are thinking about preparing for orientation in August, please keep Hub@Penn in mind. Consider sharing a link to Hub@Penn on your website where appropriate, course syllabi or course website, signature line, or any other place you think helpful. The more students using Hub@Penn, the better it becomes at meeting the Penn communities’ needs.
