Email Communication to Partners

We need your help getting the word out about Hub@Penn!

Hub@Penn was created to make Penn support resources more accessible to students, regardless of where they may be physically located.

In order for Hub@Penn to be best utilized, we need your help connecting with students. As an important part of our Penn community, you have access to students that we do not. Hub@Penn is an easy-to-use new tool that can help students meet their needs across campus: financial wellness, academic support, healthcare, career and fellowship applications, and more.

Made for students by students, Hub@Penn was created by the team of undergraduate engineers in the Penn Labs student club. Building on the success of the online platform they created for Penn Clubs, Penn Labs teamed up with New Student Orientation and Academic Initiatives, the Office of University Life, and the Online Learning Initiative to design and launch the new Hub@Penn platform.

We ask that you please include a link to Hub@Penn in communications with students where you feel appropriate (i.e. email, social media, website, etc.). We have compiled a folder of approved language and images to be used in your communications. You can find it here: https://upenn.box.com/s/7h9hxvnt89tq7nxuidxoaw5ad72sujch.

Thank you for all the hard work you do to help students. We hope that Hub@Penn will be a useful resource for you to help and direct students in finding resources to meet their needs.
